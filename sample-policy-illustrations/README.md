# Sample Policy Illustrations

Drop ClearFA / QFA policy illustration samples here for demo and extraction testing.

Use only:

- public sample policy illustrations,
- synthetic documents,
- or documents that have been fully anonymized.

Do not add real customer names, NRIC/passport numbers, phone numbers, addresses, policy numbers, adviser names, or other personal data.

PDFs in this folder are ignored by git. Commit only notes or anonymized fixtures that are safe for the whole team and deployment logs.
